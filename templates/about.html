Mission Statement

We believe that everyone deserves to know how they are changing the world we live in. In this current state of the world, new technologies are being developed to try to mitigate our damaging affects on climate change. Our helpful tool we developed allows the user to visualize the data of their own carbon footprint depending on how they change their behavior. With simple graphs that are easy to read, our program will help you make your choice towards a better future.
